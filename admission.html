Admission 2025-26

We welcome parents to enroll their children for the new academic session. Oxford Public School provides a safe and nurturing environment for holistic development.

Admission Guidelines

  • Admission is open for Nursery to Class 10.
  • Documents required: Birth Certificate, Previous School Records (if any), Passport-size photographs.
  • Forms can be submitted online or at the school office.
  • Age criteria: Nursery (3-4 yrs), LKG (4-5 yrs), UKG (5-6 yrs).
